About Us

A Nigerian Charity
Built on Compassion

Blessed Help Mission is a Nigerian charity organisation dedicated to reaching the most vulnerable in our communities — struggling families, out-of-school children, and widows who need a helping hand to get back on their feet.

We believe no child should miss school for lack of a uniform. No widow should go without food. No family should feel forgotten. Through the generosity of our donors and the dedication of our volunteers, we turn that belief into action every single day.

Providing food and essential supplies to widows and families
Paying school fees and buying uniforms for out-of-school children
Donating learning supplies to community primary schools
Annual December 31 foodstuff-sharing event for widows & families

What We Do

Our Programs Make
Real Difference

Every program directly serves the people who need it most — children, widows, and struggling families across Nigeria.

Food

01

Food & Essentials Outreach

Distributing foodstuff and essential household items to widows and struggling families, especially at our annual December 31 event.

Education

02

School Uniform Assistance

We help parents purchase school uniforms so that no child misses school simply because their family cannot afford what to wear.

School Fees

03

School Fees Support

Paying school fees for out-of-school children, giving them the chance to learn, grow, and build a better future for themselves.

Supplies

04

School Supply Donations

Visiting community primary schools to donate notebooks, pens, bags, and other learning materials so every child can learn without barriers.

Annual Event

December Outreach

Every 31st of December, Blessed Help Mission organises a special outreach to share foodstuff and essential items with widows and struggling families. It is our way of ensuring that no one enters the new year without a meal on their table.

Join us as a donor, sponsor, or volunteer to make this year's event the biggest yet. Together we can touch more lives before the new year begins.
